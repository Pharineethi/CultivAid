Government Schemes For Farmers

Plant the Seeds of Opportunity with These Schemes

Explore a range of government initiatives designed to support farmers with financial aid, resources, and knowledge. Make the most of these programs to boost agricultural growth and sustainability.

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Description: Provides direct income support of ₹6,000 annually to small and marginal farmers in three equal installments.

Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers with cultivable land.

How to Apply: Register online at PM-Kisan Portal or visit local agriculture offices.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Description: Crop insurance scheme protecting farmers against crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Eligibility: Farmers growing notified crops in notified areas.

How to Apply: Apply through the PMFBY Portal or visit Common Service Centers (CSC).

Soil Health Card Scheme

Description: Provides soil health cards to farmers with recommendations on nutrient requirements.

Eligibility: Available to all farmers in India.

How to Apply: Contact local agriculture offices for soil sample testing.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Description: Aims to improve irrigation efficiency and expand cultivated areas under irrigation.

Eligibility: Farmers with agricultural land, prioritizing low-irrigation areas.

How to Apply: Apply via PMKSY website or visit local agricultural offices.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Description: Offers credit to farmers for crop production, equipment purchase, and other needs at low-interest rates.

Eligibility: All farmers, including tenant farmers and dairy farmers.

How to Apply: Apply at banks with land records and identity proof.

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

Description: Online trading platform for creating a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

Eligibility: Farmers and traders with valid licenses.

How to Apply: Register on the e-NAM portal.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Description: Promotes organic farming through traditional and eco-friendly practices.

Eligibility: Farmers willing to adopt organic farming.

How to Apply: Apply through State Organic Farming Agencies.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Description: Provides financial assistance to states for holistic agricultural development.

Eligibility: Farmers and agricultural organizations.

How to Apply: Through state agriculture departments.
